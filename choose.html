Why Choose Us?

We are your trusted legal partner in Afghanistan, providing comprehensive, professional, and Sharia-compliant legal services to international organizations, embassies, NGOs, telecommunications companies, and commercial partners based in Afghanistan. Our legal foundation is grounded in Afghan national laws, Islamic Sharia principles, and international legal standards. Drafting, reviewing, and amending contracts across commercial, service, construction, procurement, IT, consultancy, and partnership sectors, producing balanced multilingual legal documents (English-Pashto, Dari-English, Arabic) free from usury, uncertainty, coercion, and ambiguous terms, ensuring harmony between Sharia and law in accordance with international conventions such as CISG, UNIDROIT, and UNCITRAL. Specialized legal advice for international organizations and embassies on employment contracts, confidentiality agreements, job descriptions, dispute resolution, taxation, customs, licensing, nonprofit registration, and compliance with Afghan civil, corporate, tax, labor laws, and international treaties, while respecting Islamic principles in sensitive projects. Dispute resolution through domestic and international arbitration, mediation, and judicial defense based on Afghan judiciary and international standards, including representation before primary, appellate, supreme courts, and administrative bodies, with emphasis on Sharia compliant settlements. Training programs delivered online and in-person by national and international experts in administrative, educational, and health sectors, supported by a skilled team proficient in data collection and analysis, and judicial support aligned with FIDIC, ICC, LCIA, UNCITRAL, and other international protocols. Dedicated legal services for telecommunications and technology sectors, covering licensing spectrum rights, interconnection agreements, data protection, GDPR compliance, cybersecurity laws, and marketing evaluations consistent with Islamic ethics, Afghan Telecom Law, ATRA regulations, and global IT standards. Our distinction lies in multilingual contract drafting (Pashto, Dari, English, Arabic), adherence to Hanafi Islamic jurisprudence, Afghan law, and international legal frameworks including civil and contract laws, UNCITRAL, ICC, WTO, ITU, and GDPR. We uphold values of transparency, trust, legitimacy, and principled commitment. We are committed to providing our international clients with a legal umbrella that integrates law, Sharia, professionalism, and trust—tailored to Afghanistan’s geography, customs, laws, and Islamic values. We stand as your reliable legal partner for your global mission.
