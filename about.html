ABOUT PAMA LEGAL CONSULTANCY

PAMA Legal Consultancy Service Company is a premier firm composed of highly skilled and specialized legal professionals with extensive expertise across various branches of law. Our team is dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of legal services characterized by the highest standards of professionalism and specialization. We possess deep knowledge in Sharia and Islamic jurisprudence, as well as national and international laws, enabling us to offer well-rounded and culturally informed legal advice. Our consultants combine traditional jurisprudential insights with contemporary legal practices to address complex legal challenges effectively. At PAMA, we are committed to maintaining our leadership position in the legal consultancy sector by consistently delivering superior legal services. We prioritize our clients’ interests and goals, ensuring efficient and effective solutions tailored to their unique needs. Our reputation is built on trust, expertise, and a steadfast commitment to excellence, making PAMA your reliable partner in navigating the complexities of the legal landscape.

Our Mission

PAMA Legal Consultancy Service Company, we are committed to delivering exceptional legal advisory and consultancy services to our clients with professionalism, precision, and punctuality. Our approach is grounded in deploying expert human resources, conducting thorough and authentic research and investigations, and utilizing innovative and modern work methodologies to ensure the highest standards of service and complete client satisfaction. We provide specialized legal advice and services to embassies, diplomatic delegations, United Nations organizations, and international companies based in Afghanistan, guided by the principles of Islamic Sharia and Afghan customs. Our aim is to comprehensively address their legal needs while safeguarding their rights within the framework of justice. We uphold values of integrity, professionalism, innovation, and client trust, and we are dedicated to finding effective solutions to every challenge we encounter.

Our Goals

Our primary objective is to achieve a harmonious set of goals, the most significant of which include

1. Facilitation, Coordination, and Support for the Promotion of the Rule of Law in Afghanistan We are committed to advancing the principles of justice and legal integrity, ensuring that the rule of law is upheld throughout Afghanistan in accordance with national legal frameworks, Islamic law (Sharia), and customary practices.
2. Provision of Comprehensive Legal Solutions Across Diverse Sectors We strive to deliver the most effective and inclusive legal services tailored to a broad spectrum of clients and beneficiaries, utilizing the highest professional standards to achieve optimal results in every entrusted matter.
3. Offering Legal and Advisory Services within Afghanistan’s Legal Context Our services extend to businesses, institutions, program developers, and individuals, providing expert legal counsel aligned with Afghanistan’s national legislation, Islamic jurisprudence, and customary law traditions.
4. Serving as Arbitrators and Mediators in Commercial Disputes We offer impartial arbitration and mediation services to resolve a wide variety of commercial conflicts, fostering amicable settlements that respect legal norms, religious principles, and cultural customs.
